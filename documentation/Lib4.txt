Entities Library:

	Will control both the player and the NPCs within the game and the logic that controls their 
	movement within the level. This will also handle collisions between players and NPCs. The 
	player will be relatively specific, but the NPCs will be generic so that any sort of NPC 
	can be created within the game and will follow the same logic and movements.
